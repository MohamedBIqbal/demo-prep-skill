# C-Suite Demo Script Template

**Total Duration:** 5 minutes
**Audience:** Executives, investors, technical leaders
**Format:** Video recording or live presentation

---

## Opening: The Ask First (0:00 - 0:20)

**[SLIDE 1: Title]**

**SAY:**
> "Thank you for your time.
>
> I'm building [Product Name] — [one-sentence value proposition].
>
> Today I'm asking for [specific ask #1] and [specific ask #2].
>
> Let me show you why this matters."

---

## The Pain: Make It Real (0:20 - 1:00)

**[SLIDE 2: Problem]**

**SAY:**
> "[Context about the problem domain]. [Specific requirement or constraint].
>
> But here's the reality..."

**[SLIDE 3: The Scale]**

**SAY:**
> "[Concrete example with specific numbers].
>
> [Second concrete example].
>
> [Explain the impact in business terms]."

**TRANSITION:**
> "This is what [Product Name] solves."

---

## The Solution: One Sentence (1:00 - 1:20)

**[SLIDE 4: Solution]**

**SAY:**
> "[Product Name] does one core thing: **[core value proposition]**.
>
> Under the hood, there are [N] components — [brief list].
>
> But what matters is the outcome: [outcome 1], [outcome 2], and [outcome 3]."

**TRANSITION:**
> "Let me show you."

---

## Live Demo (1:20 - 3:20)

**[SLIDE 5: Live Demo]** → **[SWITCH TO: Terminal/Interactive Demo]**

### Part 1: A Real Use Case (1:20 - 2:20)

**SAY:**
> "I'm [doing specific action]: '[specific input]'
>
> [Explain any context or constraints]."

**[RUN DEMO]**

**WHEN RESULTS APPEAR:**
> "Here's what comes back:
>
> - [Result 1] — [why it matters]
> - [Result 2] — [why it matters]
> - [Result 3] — [why it matters]
>
> [Summarize the key metric or outcome]."

### Part 2: Handling Edge Cases (2:20 - 3:20)

**SAY:**
> "Now watch what happens when [edge case scenario]."

**[SHOW EDGE CASE]**

> "[Explain what's being shown]."

**WHEN RESULTS APPEAR:**
> "[Explain how the system handled it].
>
> [Key differentiator or value demonstrated].
>
> [Business impact statement]."

**TRANSITION:**
> "That's the core value. Now let me show you where we are."

---

## Results: The Proof (3:20 - 3:50)

**[SLIDE 6: Results]**

**SAY:**
> "We've validated this with [validation method]:
>
> - [N] test scenarios covering [coverage description]
> - [N] automated tests
> - [N]% pass rate
>
> [Explain how quality is measured].
>
> [Ground the metrics in real use cases]."

---

## Honest Gaps (3:50 - 4:20)

**[SLIDE 7: Challenges]**

**SAY:**
> "Let me be direct about where this stands.
>
> [Gap 1] — [specific limitation and context].
>
> [Gap 2] — [specific limitation and what would be needed].
>
> [Gap 3] — [current state honestly described].
>
> By [timeline], I'm adding [roadmap items]."

---

## The Ask: Specific and Actionable (4:20 - 5:00)

**[SLIDE 8: The Ask]**

**SAY:**
> "Here's what I need from this group.
>
> **First: [Ask Category 1].**
> [Specific request]. [Why it matters]. [What you'd value — be specific, e.g., "30 minutes to discuss"].
>
> **Second: [Ask Category 2].**
> [Specific request]. [Who would be valuable — be specific about the role/type of person].
>
> Thank you for your time. I'm happy to take questions."

## Objection Prep (P.R.E.P. Format)

**"Is this production-ready?"**
> **Point:** [Your honest assessment].
> **Reason:** [Supporting evidence].
> **Example:** [Specific proof point].
> **Point:** [Restate with context].

**"How is this different from [competitor/alternative]?"**
> **Point:** [Key differentiator].
> **Reason:** [Why it matters].
> **Example:** [Specific demonstration of difference].
> **Point:** [Restate the value].

**"What does it cost to run?"**
> **Point:** [Cost structure overview].
> **Reason:** [Why the cost is justified].
> **Example:** [Specific metrics].
> **Point:** [Value comparison].
